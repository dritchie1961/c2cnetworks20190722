Welcome to the C2C Networks for Numismatists


The goal of C2C is to reduce middleman costs, create community and generally improve the hobby experience. 

Here are a couple examples that illustrate the MIDDLEMAN problem:


MIDDLEMAN - Auctions:  I won this beautiful top ranked token at an auction. 

$2,600.00 - Hammer price


$3,452.15 - I paid (PB + VAT) 

$2,340.00 - Seller gets (based on consignor fee of %10)


$1,112.15 - MIDDLEMAN fee!




MIDDLEMAN - Dealers:    I bought this Canadian nickel at a coin show for $400.00 and then visited the store a few days later.  The employees did not recognize me and offered me $40.00 for the coin.  I left and have not visited this store again. 




$400.00 - Show purchase price

$40.00 - Dealer would pay to buy back 

           

$360.00 - MIDDLEMAN FEE  (equivalent fee)!            


Note:  $1,200.00 - is the TRENDS price for the rotated dies variation so I thought I was getting a good price.


  

 

NO MIDDLEMAN - Collector to collector:    

 

Obviously a solution that eliminates the middleman fee in a numismatic transactions and deals with all the logistics challenges is by far the best approach. 


This is exactly what the C2C Networks is committed to providing.  The C2C ecosystem creates community and faciliates collector to collector transactions based on economic fundamentals of supply & demand.  Modest service fee may be optional or are based on the services required.  Service fees are NOT calculated based on the value of the asset - it doesn't matter if the coin is worth a $100 or $100,000. 

 

If you are tired of paying the middleman fee, and want to join a revolutionary way to pursue the hobby which you are passion ,,,, then just submit an application and give C2C a try.
